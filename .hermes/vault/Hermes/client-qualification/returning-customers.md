# Returning customers (WhatsApp)

Read with `conversation-paths.md` and `questions.md`.

A returning customer should never repeat full intake from scratch if you already know them.

## The Rule

**First-time customer:**
Name → Company → Industry → Goal → Budget → Timeline → Email → Handoff

**Returning customer:**
Greet by name → Confirm what they need now → Collect only missing fields for this project → Email (confirm prior if known) → Handoff

Never re-ask name, company, or industry if already known for the same business unless they said it is a new company.

## Scenario 1: Returning Customer - General

Customer: Hi.

Agent: Hi John. Great to hear from you again.

How have things been since we completed the website project?

Customer: Things are going well.

Agent: That's great to hear.

What can we help you with this time?

## Scenario 2: New Service, Same Business

If they want a completely new service (e.g., had website, now want marketing):

Customer: Hi. We need help with marketing now.

Agent: Hi John. Nice to hear from you again.

I can see we previously worked with Silva Holdings on your website.

What would you like to achieve with your marketing efforts?

Customer: We want more leads.

Agent: Got it.

Are you mainly looking to generate inquiries, increase brand awareness, or drive more sales?

(continue qualification for budget, timeline, email)

## Scenario 3: Prior Marketing Client, Now Wants Website

Customer: Hi. We need a new website now.

Agent: Hi Nadeesha. Nice to hear from you again.

I remember we previously discussed content marketing for your business.

What are you hoping the new website will help you achieve?

Customer: More inquiries.

Agent: That makes sense.

Would you like the website to mainly generate leads, build trust with potential customers, or sell products online?

(continue qualification)

## Scenario 4: Coming Back After Months

Customer: Hi.

Agent: Hi Ruwan. Welcome back.

It's been a while since we last spoke.

How has business been?

Customer: Pretty good.

Agent: Glad to hear that.

What can we help you with today?

## Scenario 5: Not Sure If Same Project or New

Customer: Hi again.

Agent: Hi John. Great to hear from you again.

Are you reaching out about your existing project, or is this something new you'd like help with?

## Scenario 6: Known Profile, New Project Type

If you already know their name, company, and industry:

Agent: Welcome back, John.

I still have Silva Holdings listed as a construction company.

What would you like help with this time?

Customer: We need social media marketing.

Agent: Got it.

What are you hoping to achieve through marketing?

(continue — budget, timeline, email confirm)

## Same Ongoing Project

If they confirm it is about the existing project, acknowledge and hand off to the founder. Do not re-run intake.

## Email on Return

If you have their email from before:
should we use [email] again to reach you on this one?

Only ask for a new email if they say no.
